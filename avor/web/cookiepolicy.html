Cookie Policy

How AVOR handles cookies and consent.

Cookies

Cookies are small text files a site stores in your browser to remember things between visits — a language choice, a session, a saved preference. Related technologies such as local storage, pixels, tags, and embedded services can serve similar purposes. AVOR uses only strictly necessary storage by default. Optional storage is used only when you choose to allow it.

Categories

Necessary

Required for the site to work and to remember your consent choice. This category is always active and is not used for advertising or audience profiling.

Functional

Optional for remembering preference and enabling conveniences like saved display settings, region choices, and embedded content.

Analytics

Optional to measure aggregate usage, including page views, navigation paths, and interaction patterns, to understand how the site is used.

Performance

Optional for load-speed and error monitoring to improve the experience.

Marketing

Optional for marketing measurement, campaign attribution, and relevance based on browsing activity.

Storage details

cookie_consent

Necessary for storing your cookie preference locally so the site can respect your choice until you clear site data or update your preference. Provided by AVOR, no third-party access.

avor_locale

Functional for remembering language and regional display preferences. Provided by AVOR, stored for 12 months, and no third-party access.

avor_analytics_id

Analytics that help count visits and understand journeys across AVOR pages without identifying you by your name. Provided by AVOR, stored for 12 months, and no third-party access.

avor_perf_session

Performance analytics for measuring page load timing and client-side errors for a single browsing session. Provided by AVOR, stored for one session, and no third-party access.

avor_campaign

Marketing analysis for measuring campaign source and frequency so marketing is not repeated unnecessarily. Provided by AVOR, stored for 3 months, accessed by approved measurement partners when approved.

Managing your choices

Cookie Preferences

Open Cookie Preferences from the footer of any AVOR page to accept all or keep only the necessary set. Your choice is stored locally and can be changed at any time with effect for future visits. You can also clear or block cookies in your browser settings.

Legal basis

Necessary storage supports the website and remembers your consent choice. Functional, analytics, performance, and marketing storage are based on your consent. You can withdraw consent at any time for future visits.

Contact

AVOR is responsible for this website and its cookie choices. If you have questions about this policy reach out at privacy@avor.com.
