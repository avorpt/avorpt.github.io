Cookie Policy

How AVOR handles cookies and consent.

Cookies

Cookies are small text files a site stores in your browser to remember things between visits — a language choice, a session, a saved preference. Related technologies such as local storage, pixels, tags, and embedded services can serve similar purposes. AVOR uses only strictly necessary storage by default. Optional storage is used only when you choose to allow it.

This page describes the consent model used for AVOR web experiences.

Categories

Necessary. Required for the site to work and to remember your consent choice. This category is always active and is not used for advertising or audience profiling.

Functional. Optional. Remembers preferences and enables conveniences like saved display settings, region choices, and embedded content.

Analytics. Optional. Measures aggregate usage, including page views, navigation paths, and interaction patterns, to understand how the site is used.

Performance. Optional. Load-speed and error monitoring to improve the experience.

Marketing. Optional. Marketing measurement, campaign attribution, and relevance based on browsing activity.

Storage details

cookie-consent. Necessary. Stores your cookie preference locally so the site can respect your choice. Provider: AVOR. Duration: until you clear site data or update your preference. Third-party access: no.

avor_locale. Functional. Remembers language and regional display preferences. Provider: AVOR. Duration: 12 months. Third-party access: no.

avor_analytics_id. Analytics. Helps count visits and understand journeys across AVOR pages without identifying you by name. Provider: AVOR analytics service. Duration: 13 months. Third-party access: processor access only.

avor_perf_session. Performance. Measures page load timing and client-side errors for a single browsing session. Provider: AVOR performance service. Duration: session. Third-party access: processor access only.

avor_campaign. Marketing. Measures campaign source and frequency so marketing is not repeated unnecessarily. Provider: AVOR marketing service. Duration: 90 days. Third-party access: approved measurement partners where enabled.

Managing your choices

Open Cookie Preferences from the footer of any AVOR page to accept all or keep only the necessary set. Your choice is stored locally and can be changed at any time with effect for future visits. You can also clear or block cookies in your browser settings.

Legal basis

Necessary storage supports the website and remembers your consent choice. Functional, analytics, performance, and marketing storage are based on your consent. You can withdraw consent at any time for future visits.

AVOR is responsible for this website and its cookie choices. Questions about this policy? Reach out at privacy@avor.com.
