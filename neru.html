Story

Neru.

A controlled experiment outside the AVOR context.
A fictional wellness brand — built in hours to test whether the process holds when the domain, the tone, and the visual register all change at once.

AVOR is geometric, dark, sans-serif, restrained. Neru needed the opposite — serif typography, warmer palette, curves instead of hard geometry, a voice that creates space rather than holding it. The exercise was deliberate; same process, different constraints, observed from outside.

Project

Gently Guiding.

The brand does not talk about emotions.
It creates conditions where they become legible.

The process mostly held. Brand positioning, voice, color, and typography transferred as method even when every surface decision changed. The logo never fully resolved — the difficulty of working with curves and softness after months inside a geometric system was the gap the exercise was designed to find. It found it.
